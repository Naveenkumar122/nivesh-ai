What is an ETF?

An Exchange-Traded Fund (ETF) is a basket of stocks that trades on the stock exchange just like a regular stock. When you buy 1 unit of NIFTYBEES, you're essentially buying a tiny piece of all 50 NIFTY 50 companies at once.

The Monthly SIP Strategy

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) means investing a fixed amount every month, regardless of market conditions. This is the single most powerful strategy for beginners.

Rupee Cost Averaging

When market is high, you buy fewer units. When market drops, you buy more units. Over time, your average cost is lower than the average price.

Removes Emotions

No need to time the market. No panic selling. No FOMO buying. Just a fixed amount, every month, automatically.

Power of Compounding

Rs.5,000/month at 12% for 20 years = Rs.49.9 lakh. You only invested Rs.12 lakh. The rest is compounding.
